No.31 Next Permutation

https://leetcode.com/problems/next-permutation/

analysis

The most important part is to find the critical point which is the first point that show increasing tendency from right side. After finding that point, we need to find the first point bigger than that critical point and exchange them. For the other points on the right side of the critical point, we just reverse them. To prove that the algorithm is optimal. The critical point is found from right side, which is correponding to the lower digit. Also, the first number bigger from right side than that critical point is also the smallest number but bigger than critical number since we pass them with the condition that $nums[right-1]>=nums[right]$. The running time can be bounded to $O(n)â€‹$.
